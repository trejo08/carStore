Quines Somos

If home is where your heart is, our Studio is our Home. Warm brick, soaring heights and flooded with light, our Studio provides an inspirational workspace and beautiful showroom. Filled with our Italian Leather Boots, Handmade Leather Bags and Leather Goods, you're welcome to shop or simply enjoy the scenery.

We also offer our Vintage Finds - treasures from afar to take home and fall in love with. Curl up on one of the vintage chesterfields, or enjoy a caffe’ in one of the Parisian club chairs. We invite you to linger.

In the evenings, we often host friends and family for nights filled with food, drinks and incredible music by talented musicians who grace our stage. Check out our Facebook page or join our mailing list below to be notified of upcoming events.

This is our Studio. Welcome home.
