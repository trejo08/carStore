Registro de Usuario

What started out as a passion for fine leather craftsmanship soon grew into the revival of a family trade. It was only after we moved to Italy to immerse ourselves in the shoemaking craft that we found out my grandfather, whom I never knew, was in fact a shoemaker from Italy. After realizing this, we were compelled to build this business based on the qualities from generations past. Character, Authenticity and Timelessness resonate in every business decision, every design, every relationship we have.

It's a privilege to continue the craft and journey Peter Nappi began over a century earlier. To see these goods become real is almost too humbling for us to grasp. For this reason, we will always be relentlessly devoted to the quality of our goods, the welfare of the artisans, and our responsibility to clients.

This is my heritage, I am just happy to be able to carry it on.

Welcome to Peter Nappi. Born 1887, est. 2009.
